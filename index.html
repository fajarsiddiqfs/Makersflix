FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

How do I get started?
It's simple — book a free call, share your content goals, and upload your first footage. We'll match you with a dedicated editor and have your first video ready within 48–72 hours.
Is my footage safe and private?
Yes. Your footage is encrypted in transit and only accessible by your dedicated editing team. We never share or use client content for any other purpose.
How fast will I get my videos back?
Starter plan clients receive videos within 48–72 hours. Growth and Enterprise clients get priority turnaround, often same-day for urgent requests.
What if I don't like the edit?
Every plan includes revisions — Starter gets 1 per video, Growth gets 2, and Enterprise gets unlimited. We work until you're happy with it.
Do you work with brands or just solo creators?
Both. We work with solo creators, influencers, SMMA owners and growing brands. Whether you need 4 videos a month or 40, we have a plan that fits.
