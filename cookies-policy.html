Cookies Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  MakersFlix by Euphorya

We use cookies to make MakersFlix work properly and to understand how people use our site. This page explains what cookies we use and why.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit — like your preferences or how you interact with the page — making your next visit smoother and the site more useful to you.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
_session Essential Maintains your session state across pages Session
_ga, _gid Analytics Google Analytics — tracks anonymous usage data Up to 2 years
cal_* Third-party Cal.com booking widget — enables scheduling Session
__stripe_* Third-party Stripe — fraud prevention and payment flow Up to 1 year

3. Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality. No personal data is stored in essential cookies.

4. Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with MakersFlix. All data collected is anonymous and aggregated — we cannot identify individual users from analytics data.

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General location (country/city level only)
  • Device type, browser, and referral source

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services embedded in our site. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing the privacy policies of each service:

  • Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Cal.com Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here's how to manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of MakersFlix, including the booking system and payment processing.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy as we add new features or third-party services. Check back here periodically or see the "Last updated" date above.
